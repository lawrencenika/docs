## Enterprise Features

Built for organizations that need enterprise-grade geospatial solutions.

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card
    title="Advanced Geoprocessing"
    icon="tree"
  >
    Built-in functions that match ArcGIS tools, including pansharpening, superresolution, SAM2, acolite, buffer, centroid, and 200+ more geoprocessing operations for professional GIS workflows.
  </Card>
  <Card
    title="Single Sign-On (SSO)"
    icon="shield-check"
  >
    Seamless integration with your organization's Active Directory, LDAP, or other identity providers. Enable secure team collaboration with centralized authentication and role-based access control.
  </Card>
  <Card
    title="Data Migration Support"
    icon="cloud-arrow-up"
  >
    Dedicated support engineers to help migrate terabytes of data from any cloud service provider (AWS, Azure, GCP) or desktop systems. Get expert assistance for complex data transfers and format conversions.
  </Card>
  <Card
    title="On-Premise Deployment"
    icon="server"
  >
    Deploy Nika on your own infrastructure for maximum data sovereignty and compliance. Full control over your geospatial data with enterprise-grade security, backup, and disaster recovery capabilities.
